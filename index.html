Privacy Policy for Plurality

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Plurality is designed so that your data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Plurality does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts and no login.

Where Your Data Lives

Everything you enter is stored locally on your device. This includes members, groups, fronting history, journal entries, notes, group channels and direct messages, polls, reminders, custom fields, and settings. We operate no servers and have no access to your data.

Backups and Sync

If you choose to create a backup or sync your data using files, those files are created and stored by you. For example, you might save them to a folder or a cloud storage location that you select. The app does not send your data to us or to any third party. You can encrypt backups with a passphrase that only you control.

Photos

If you set a member avatar from your photo library, the selected image is stored locally with your other app data. It is not uploaded anywhere.

Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications, such as reminders, on your device. These notifications are generated and delivered locally, and they do not pass through any server.

Analytics and Tracking

Plurality contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking by any third party.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
