Privacy Policy for Plurality

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Plurality (“the app”) is a local-first application. It is designed so that your data stays with you.

Information We Collect

Plurality does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts and no login.

Where Your Data Lives

Everything you enter — members, fronting history, journal entries, notes, polls, reminders, custom fields, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We operate no servers and have no access to your data.

Backups and Sync

If you choose to create a backup or use file-based sync, those files are created and stored by you (for example, in a folder or cloud-storage location you select). The app does not send your data to us or to any third party. Backups can be encrypted with a passphrase that only you control.

Photos

If you set a member avatar from your photo library, the selected image is stored locally alongside your other app data. It is not uploaded anywhere.

Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications (such as reminders) on your device. These are generated and delivered locally and do not pass through any server.

Analytics and Tracking

Plurality contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.
